Hi, I’m Praneeth Ganesh—a full stack developer who loves building modern web and mobile solutions. I specialize in React for engaging interfaces, Spring Boot for solid backend structure, and innovative work with AI and 3D modeling. My projects blend technical skill and creativity to bring digital ideas to life.

My toolkit includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Java, Spring Boot, and MySQL. I constantly explore advanced areas such as artificial intelligence, computer vision, and gamification, striving to create products that are both practical and user-friendly. I have joined college internships and events, collaborating with others and sharpening my problem-solving skills while learning real-world software development.
